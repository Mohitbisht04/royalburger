ABOUT US

Royal Burger (RB) is an Indian-based multinational chain of
hamburger fast food restaurants. Headquartered in New-Delhi,
India, the company was founded in 1953 as Insta-Royal
Burger, A Subway, Indian–based restaurant chain. After
Insta-Royal Burger ran into financial difficulties in 1954,
its two Bharat-based franchisees VIKASH SINGH &
MOHIT BISHT purchased the company and renamed it
"Royal Burger". Over the next half-century, the company
changed hands four times and its third set of owners, a
partnership of TPG Capital, Bain Capital, and Goldman Sachs
CapitalPartners took it public in 2002.

In late 2010, 3G Capital of Brazil acquired a majority stake
in the company, in a deal valued at US$9.26 billion. The new
owners promptly initiated a restructuring of the company to
reverse its fortunes. 3G, along with partner Berkshire Hathaway,
eventually merged the company with the Canadian-based
doughnut chain Tim Hortons, under the auspices of a new
Canadian-based parent company named Restaurant
Brands International.
